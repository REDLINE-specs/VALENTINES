Letter 10 – Sweet Reflections

Sometimes I catch myself smiling for no reason, and then I realize it’s you. Your laughter echoes in my mind like a favorite melody, and your touch lingers in my memory long after we part. Every little gesture, every whispered word, reminds me how lucky I am to share this life with you.

I hope that no matter where life leads us, we can always return to this quiet space of understanding, where just being together is enough to feel infinite.
