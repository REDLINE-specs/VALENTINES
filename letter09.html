Letter 09 – Biblical Love

My love, “Love is patient, love is kind. It does not envy, it does not boast, it is not proud.” – 1 Corinthians 13:4 Every time I read these words, I think of us. Your patience, your kindness, and the way you care for my heart remind me that true love is a gift, and I am endlessly grateful for you.

I promise to honor this love, to grow together in faith and joy, and to always keep you close to my heart, my dearest.
